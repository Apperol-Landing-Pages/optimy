Optimy Cleaner – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes in extensive detail how Optimy Cleaner ("Optimy Cleaner", "we", "us", "our") collects, accesses, processes, protects, and limits the use of information when you use the Optimy Cleaner mobile application (the "Application" or "Service"). This document is intentionally comprehensive and verbose in order to meet high transparency standards and platform requirements.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or otherwise using the Application, you acknowledge that you have carefully read, fully understood, and expressly agreed to all terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Policy Purpose, Legal Scope, and Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies globally to all users of the Optimy Cleaner Application, regardless of country, device model, or operating system version. It governs all personal and non-personal information processing activities carried out in connection with the Service.

This Policy applies to all features, tools, subscriptions, free trials, communications, and support interactions related to Optimy Cleaner. If you do not agree with any portion of this Policy, you must immediately discontinue use of the Application.

2. Detailed Description of the Optimy Cleaner Service

Optimy Cleaner is a device optimization and maintenance utility designed to help users manage digital clutter and improve overall device efficiency. The Application analyzes user-selected data in order to provide actionable insights and cleanup tools.

  • Detection of duplicate photos based on metadata and visual similarity
  • Detection of visually similar images using on-device analysis
  • Identification of duplicate, oversized, or unnecessary videos
  • Storage usage visualization and insights
  • Removal of unwanted files selected by the user
  • Detection and merging of duplicate contacts
  • Management and cleanup of outdated calendar events
  • Recommendations intended to reduce device overheating
  • Recommendations intended to reduce excessive battery consumption

All optimization suggestions are advisory only and do not constitute technical guarantees.

3. Fundamental Privacy Principles

Optimy Cleaner is built with a strong privacy-first mindset. Our internal design and engineering decisions are guided by the following principles:

  • Collect only the minimum amount of data required to operate
  • Prefer local, on-device processing whenever technically feasible
  • Avoid long-term storage of user content
  • Provide clear permission explanations
  • Never sell or rent personal data

4. Information You Choose to Provide

You may voluntarily provide limited personal information when contacting us. This typically includes your email address and the content of your message.

This information is used exclusively for responding to inquiries, providing technical assistance, resolving issues, and improving customer support quality.

5. Permissions and On-Device Data Access

Certain features require access to on-device data. Granting permissions is entirely optional and can be managed at any time via iOS system settings.

5.1 Photos and Videos

If permission is granted, the Application may scan photo and video metadata and thumbnails to detect duplicates, similarities, and storage usage patterns. All such analysis is performed locally on your device.

Optimy Cleaner does not upload, transmit, or store your photos or videos on external servers.

5.2 Contacts

If contact access is granted, the Application may analyze contact records to identify duplicates or incomplete entries. All processing remains local to your device.

5.3 Calendar

If calendar access is granted, the Application may review events to help identify outdated or redundant items. Calendar data is never stored externally.

6. Automatically Collected Technical Information

To ensure stability, performance, and continuous improvement, the Application may automatically collect anonymized technical and diagnostic information.

  • Device model and hardware configuration
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Anonymous feature usage metrics
  • Crash logs and error diagnostics

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

7. Subscription, Free Trial, and Payment Processing

Optimy Cleaner offers a three-day free trial followed by optional paid subscriptions. All billing operations are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store.

We do not collect or store payment card numbers, bank details, or Apple ID credentials. We only receive limited subscription status confirmations from Apple.

8. Purposes and Legal Bases for Processing

Information is processed solely for legitimate operational purposes, including enabling functionality, improving performance, maintaining security, and complying with legal obligations.

9. Data Retention Practices

User content such as photos, videos, contacts, and calendar events remains on the user’s device and is not retained by Optimy Cleaner.

Technical data is retained only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes.

10. Data Security Measures

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

11. User Rights and Regulatory Protections

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws, including rights of access, correction, deletion, and objection.

12. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

13. International Data Considerations

Technical information may be processed in different jurisdictions with appropriate legal safeguards in place.

14. Performance and Optimization Disclaimer

Optimy Cleaner provides recommendations intended to improve storage availability, reduce overheating, and improve battery efficiency. Results may vary.

15. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the updated version.

By continuing to use Optimy Cleaner, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
